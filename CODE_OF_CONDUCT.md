# 🌟 PlumoAI Community Code of Conduct

Welcome to **PlumoAI** 🚀
We are building the future of **Autonomous AI Employees** together.

Our community includes developers, founders, researchers, and builders from around the world 🌍.
To keep this ecosystem productive, safe, and collaborative, we follow the principles below.

---

# 🤝 Our Community Promise

We are committed to creating a **respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment** for everyone.

This means:

✅ Respect different opinions and perspectives
✅ Encourage constructive discussions
✅ Help new contributors learn and grow
✅ Build technology responsibly

PlumoAI is not just code.
It is a **global builder community**.

---

# 💡 Expected Behavior

To keep the community healthy, we expect everyone to:

### 🧠 Be respectful

Treat others with kindness and professionalism.

### 💬 Communicate constructively

Give feedback that helps improve ideas and code.

### 🙌 Support contributors

Encourage new developers joining the ecosystem.

### 🔍 Focus on solutions

Discuss problems in a productive way.

### 🌍 Respect global diversity

People from many cultures collaborate here.

---

# 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not allowed in the PlumoAI community:

❌ Harassment or discrimination
❌ Personal attacks or abusive language
❌ Hate speech or offensive content
❌ Trolling or intentionally disrupting discussions
❌ Sharing private information without permission
❌ Spamming or promotional abuse

If someone violates these rules, maintainers may take action.

---

# 🛠 Where This Code Applies

This Code of Conduct applies to all official PlumoAI spaces:

📂 GitHub repositories
💬 GitHub Discussions
🐞 Issue tracking
📢 Community forums
🎤 Events or meetups related to PlumoAI

If you represent PlumoAI anywhere, these rules apply.

---

# 🚨 Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

📧 Contact the maintainers
Open a confidential report to the project maintainers.

Maintainers will review the issue and take appropriate action.

We will always prioritize **community safety and fairness**.

---

# ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers of PlumoAI are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Possible actions include:

⚠️ Warning the contributor
🔇 Temporary suspension
🚫 Permanent ban from the community

All decisions will be made to maintain a **healthy ecosystem for builders**.
